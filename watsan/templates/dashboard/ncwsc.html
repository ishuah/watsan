What you need for your application:

  • Copy of ID
  • Copy of PIN Certificate
  • Passport Photo
  • Proof of Land Ownership. e.g. Title Deed, LR No. of plot, Letter from Chief
  • Map/Sketch of your project site with nearby landmarks

Steps to Connect

  1. Go to NCWSC Office to fill out application
  2. Submit application and pay processing fee
  3. NCWSC sends team to verify location and connectability
  4. Customer Care creates account and Applicant installs new pipes with advice from NCWSC
